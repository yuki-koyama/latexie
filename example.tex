Gaussian distribution:
\begin{align}
  \calN(\bfx; \bfmu, \bfSigma)
  =
  \frac{1}{(2 \pi)^{\frac{n}{2}} \det(\bfSigma)^{\frac{1}{2}}}
  \exp \left\{
    - \frac{1}{2} (\bfx - \bfmu)^\T \bfSigma^\inv (\bfx - \bfmu)
  \right\}.
\end{align}
